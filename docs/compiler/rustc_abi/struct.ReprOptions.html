Fields§

§int: Option<IntegerType>§align: Option<Align>§pack: Option<Align>§flags: ReprFlags§field_shuffle_seed: Hash64

The seed to be used for randomizing a type’s layout

Note: This could technically be a Hash128 which would be the “most accurate” hash as it’d encompass the item and crate hash without loss, but it does pay the price of being larger. Everything’s a tradeoff, a 64-bit seed should be sufficient for our purposes (primarily -Z randomize-layout)
