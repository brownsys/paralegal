Base local of the place we are collecting regions for.

§place_stack: Vec<PlaceElem<'tcx>>

List of projections to apply to the base local in order to reach the child place currently under consideration.

Starts out as the projections in the input place.

Sequence of parent types to reach the place currently under consideration. Correspond to the projections in place_stack.
